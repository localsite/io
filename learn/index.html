2021 Web Challenge


The 2021 Web Challenge is open to K-12 and college students, coders, designers and community reps.

Created with the US EPA's new input-output model, the website features maps and data visualizations that combine industry and environmental data using Embeddable IO Widgets.

Our Industry Comparison Tools answer questions like: What are the top industries in my community? How do these industries impact air, water, land, energy use, job creation, health and prosperity? Where can we focus innovation for the greatest returns?
